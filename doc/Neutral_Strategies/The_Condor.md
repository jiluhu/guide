# Condor Options


# 神鹰选项
The condor option strategy is a limited risk, non-directional option trading strategy that is structured to earn a limited profit when the         underlying security is perceived to have little volatility. 

b'\xe7\xa7\x83\xe9\xb9\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xe6\x98\xaf\xe4\xb8\x80\xe7\xa7\x8d\xe6\x9c\x89\xe9\x99\x90\xe9\xa3\x8e\xe9\x99\xa9\xe7\x9a\x84\xe9\x9d\x9e\xe5\xae\x9a\xe5\x90\x91\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\x85\xb6\xe7\xbb\x93\xe6\x9e\x84\xe6\x98\xaf\xe5\xbd\x93\xe5\x9f\xba\xe7\xa1\x80\xe8\xaf\x81\xe5\x88\xb8\xe8\xa2\xab\xe8\xae\xa4\xe4\xb8\xba\xe5\x85\xb7\xe6\x9c\x89\xe5\xbe\x88\xe5\xb0\x8f\xe7\x9a\x84\xe6\xb3\xa2\xe5\x8a\xa8\xe6\x80\xa7\xe6\x97\xb6\xe8\x8e\xb7\xe5\xbe\x97\xe6\x9c\x89\xe9\x99\x90\xe7\x9a\x84\xe5\x88\xa9\xe6\xb6\xa6\xe3\x80\x82'
Using call options expiring on the same month, the trader can implement a long condor option spread by writing a lower strike in-the-money call,        buying an even lower striking in-the-money call, writing a higher strike out-of-the-money call        and buying another even higher striking out-of-the-money call. A total of 4 legs are involved in the condor options strategy and a net debit is required to establish the position.

b'\xe4\xbd\xbf\xe7\x94\xa8\xe5\x90\x8c\xe4\xb8\x80\xe4\xb8\xaa\xe6\x9c\x88\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe7\x9a\x84\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe6\xb6\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe8\x80\x85\xe5\x8f\xaf\xe4\xbb\xa5\xe9\x80\x9a\xe8\xbf\x87\xe5\x86\x99\xe4\xb8\x80\xe4\xb8\xaa\xe8\xbe\x83\xe4\xbd\x8e\xe7\x9a\x84\xe7\xbd\xa2\xe5\xb7\xa5\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\x86\x85\xe9\x80\x9a\xe8\xaf\x9d\xe5\xae\x9e\xe7\x8e\xb0\xe9\x95\xbf\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe7\xa7\x83\xe9\xb9\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\xb7\xae\xef\xbc\x8c\xe8\xb4\xad\xe4\xb9\xb0\xe4\xb8\x80\xe4\xb8\xaa\xe6\x9b\xb4\xe4\xbd\x8e\xe7\x9a\x84\xe6\x83\x8a\xe4\xba\xba\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\x86\x85\xe9\x80\x9a\xe8\xaf\x9d\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\x86\x99\xe5\x87\xba\xe6\x9b\xb4\xe9\xab\x98\xe7\x9a\x84\xe7\xbd\xa2\xe5\xb7\xa5'
## Limited Profit


## 有限的利润
Maximum profit for the long condor option strategy is achieved when the stock price falls between the 2 middle    strikes at expiration. It can be derived that the maximum profit is equal to the difference in strike prices    of the 2 lower striking calls less    the initial debit taken to    enter the trade.

b'\xe5\xbd\x93\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe6\xa0\xbc\xe5\x9c\xa8\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x97\xb6\xe7\x9a\x84\xe4\xb8\xa4\xe6\xac\xa1\xe4\xb8\xad\xe9\x97\xb4\xe7\xbd\xa2\xe5\xb7\xa5\xe4\xb9\x8b\xe9\x97\xb4\xe4\xb8\x8b\xe9\x99\x8d\xe6\x97\xb6\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\xae\x9e\xe7\x8e\xb0\xe9\x95\xbf\xe7\xa7\x83\xe5\xad\x90\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xe7\x9a\x84\xe6\x9c\x80\xe5\xa4\xa7\xe5\x88\xa9\xe6\xb6\xa6\xe3\x80\x82'

## Limited Risk


## 风险有限
    The maximum possible loss for a long condor option strategy is equal to the initial debit taken when    entering the trade. It happens when the underlying stock price on expiration date    is at or below the lowest strike price and also occurs when the stock price is at    or above the highest strike price of all the options involved.

b'\xe9\x95\xbf\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe7\xa7\x83\xe9\xb9\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xe7\x9a\x84\xe6\x9c\x80\xe5\xa4\xa7\xe5\x8f\xaf\xe8\x83\xbd\xe6\x8d\x9f\xe5\xa4\xb1\xe7\xad\x89\xe4\xba\x8e\xe8\xbf\x9b\xe5\x85\xa5\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe6\x97\xb6\xe7\x9a\x84\xe5\x88\x9d\xe5\xa7\x8b\xe5\x80\x9f\xe8\xae\xb0\xe3\x80\x82'

## Example


## 例
    Suppose XYZ stock is trading at $45 in June. An options trader enters a condor trade by buying a JUL 35 call for $1100, writing a JUL 40 call for $700, writing another    JUL 50 call for $200 and buying another JUL 55 call for $100. The net debit required    to enter the trade is $300, which is also his maximum possible loss.

b'\xe5\x81\x87\xe8\xae\xbeXYZ\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa86\xe6\x9c\x88\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe6\xa0\xbc\xe4\xb8\xba45\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe3\x80\x82'
        To further see why $300 is the maximum possible loss, lets examine what happens        when the stock price falls to $35     or rise to $55 on expiration.    

b'\xe4\xb8\xba\xe4\xba\x86\xe8\xbf\x9b\xe4\xb8\x80\xe6\xad\xa5\xe4\xba\x86\xe8\xa7\xa3\xe4\xb8\xba\xe4\xbb\x80\xe4\xb9\x88300\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe6\x98\xaf\xe6\x9c\x80\xe5\xa4\xa7\xe5\x8f\xaf\xe8\x83\xbd\xe7\x9a\x84\xe6\x8d\x9f\xe5\xa4\xb1\xef\xbc\x8c\xe8\xae\xa9\xe6\x88\x91\xe4\xbb\xac\xe6\x9d\xa5\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe5\xbd\x93\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe6\xa0\xbc\xe8\xb7\x8c\xe8\x87\xb335\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe6\x88\x96\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x97\xb6\xe5\x8d\x87\xe8\x87\xb355\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe6\x97\xb6\xe4\xbc\x9a\xe5\x8f\x91\xe7\x94\x9f\xe4\xbb\x80\xe4\xb9\x88\xe3\x80\x82'
        At $35, all the options expire worthless, so the initial debit taken of $300 is        his maximum loss.    

b'35\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe6\x89\x80\xe6\x9c\x89\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe9\x83\xbd\xe6\xb2\xa1\xe6\x9c\x89\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\x80\xbc\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\x9b\xa0\xe6\xad\xa4\xe6\x9c\x80\xe5\x88\x9d\xe7\x9a\x84300\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe5\x80\x9f\xe8\xae\xb0\xe6\x98\xaf\xe4\xbb\x96\xe7\x9a\x84\xe6\x9c\x80\xe5\xa4\xa7\xe6\x8d\x9f\xe5\xa4\xb1\xe3\x80\x82'
        At $55, the long JUL 55 call expires worthless while the long JUL 35 call worth        $2000 is used to offset the loss from the short JUL 40 call (worth $1500) and the        short JUL 50 call (worth $500). Thus, the long condor trader still suffers the maximum        loss that is equal to the $300 initial debit taken when entering the trade.    

b'\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\x80\xbc55\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe9\x95\xbf\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe7\x9a\x84JUL 55\xe9\x80\x9a\xe8\xaf\x9d\xe6\x97\xa0\xe6\x95\x88\xef\xbc\x8c\xe8\x80\x8c\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\x80\xbc2000\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe7\x9a\x84\xe9\x95\xbf\xe6\x9c\x9fJUL 35\xe9\x80\x9a\xe8\xaf\x9d\xe7\x94\xa8\xe4\xba\x8e\xe6\x8a\xb5\xe6\xb6\x88\xe7\x9f\xad\xe6\x9c\x9fJUL 40\xe9\x80\x9a\xe8\xaf\x9d\xef\xbc\x88\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\x80\xbc1500\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x89\xe5\x92\x8c\xe7\x9f\xad\xe6\x9c\x9fJUL 50\xe9\x80\x9a\xe8\xaf\x9d\xef\xbc\x88\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\x80\xbc500\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x89\xe7\x9a\x84\xe6\x8d\x9f\xe5\xa4\xb1\xe3\x80\x82'
        If instead on expiration in July, XYZ stock is still trading at $45, only the        JUL 35 call and the JUL 40 call expires in the money. With his long JUL 35 call worth        $1000 to offset the short JUL 40 call valued at $500 and the initial debit        of $300, his net profit comes to $200.    

b'\xe5\xa6\x82\xe6\x9e\x9c\xe5\x9c\xa87\xe6\x9c\x88\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x97\xb6\xef\xbc\x8cXYZ\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe4\xbb\x8d\xe7\x84\xb6\xe5\x9c\xa845\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\x8f\xaa\xe6\x9c\x89JUL 35\xe7\x94\xb5\xe8\xaf\x9d\xe5\x92\x8cJUL 40\xe7\x94\xb5\xe8\xaf\x9d\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe3\x80\x82'
        The maximum profit for the condor trade may be low in relation to other trading strategies        but it has a comparatively wider profit zone. In this example, maximum profit is        achieved if the underlying stock price at expiration is anywhere between $40 and        $50.     

b'\xe4\xb8\x8e\xe5\x85\xb6\xe4\xbb\x96\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xe7\x9b\xb8\xe6\xaf\x94\xef\xbc\x8c\xe7\xa5\x9e\xe9\xb9\xb0\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe7\x9a\x84\xe6\x9c\x80\xe5\xa4\xa7\xe5\x88\xa9\xe6\xb6\xa6\xe5\x8f\xaf\xe8\x83\xbd\xe8\xbe\x83\xe4\xbd\x8e\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xbd\x86\xe5\x85\xb6\xe5\x88\xa9\xe6\xb6\xa6\xe5\x8c\xba\xe7\x9b\xb8\xe5\xaf\xb9\xe8\xbe\x83\xe5\xae\xbd\xe3\x80\x82'
Note: While we have covered the use of this strategy with reference to stock options, the condor is equally applicable using ETF options, index options as well as options on futures.

b'\xe6\xb3\xa8\xe6\x84\x8f\xef\xbc\x9a\xe8\x99\xbd\xe7\x84\xb6\xe6\x88\x91\xe4\xbb\xac\xe5\xb7\xb2\xe7\xbb\x8f\xe5\x8f\x82\xe8\x80\x83\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe6\xb6\xb5\xe7\x9b\x96\xe4\xba\x86\xe6\xad\xa4\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xe7\x9a\x84\xe4\xbd\xbf\xe7\x94\xa8\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xbd\x86\xe7\xa5\x9e\xe9\xb9\xb0\xe5\x90\x8c\xe6\xa0\xb7\xe9\x80\x82\xe7\x94\xa8\xe4\xba\x8eETF\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe6\x8c\x87\xe6\x95\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe4\xbb\xa5\xe5\x8f\x8a\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe8\xb4\xa7\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe3\x80\x82'
## Commissions


## 委员会

## The Short Condor


## 短秃鹰
The converse strategy to the long condor is the short condor. Short condor    spreads are used when one perceives the volatility of the price of the underlying    stock to be high.

b'\xe5\xaf\xb9\xe9\x95\xbf\xe7\xa7\x83\xe5\xa4\xb4\xe7\x9a\x84\xe9\x80\x86\xe5\x90\x91\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xe6\x98\xaf\xe7\x9f\xad\xe7\xa7\x83\xe9\xb9\xb0\xe3\x80\x82'
## The Iron Condor


## 铁神鹰
There exists a slightly different version of the long condor strategy which is known        as the iron condor. It is entered with a credit instead of a debit and involve less commission charges.
