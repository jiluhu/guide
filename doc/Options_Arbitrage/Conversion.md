# Conversion


# 转变
    A conversion is an arbitrage strategy in options trading that can be performed for    a riskless profit when options are overpriced relative to the         underlying stock.    To do a conversion, the trader buys the underlying stock and offset it with an equivalent     synthetic short stock (long put + short call) position.

b'\xe8\xbd\xac\xe6\x8d\xa2\xe6\x98\xaf\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe4\xb8\xad\xe7\x9a\x84\xe5\xa5\x97\xe5\x88\xa9\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\xbd\x93\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe7\x9b\xb8\xe5\xaf\xb9\xe4\xba\x8e\xe5\xba\x95\xe5\xb1\x82\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe5\xae\x9a\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe8\xbf\x87\xe9\xab\x98\xe6\x97\xb6\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\x8f\xaf\xe4\xbb\xa5\xe6\x89\xa7\xe8\xa1\x8c\xe6\x97\xa0\xe9\xa3\x8e\xe9\x99\xa9\xe5\x88\xa9\xe6\xb6\xa6\xe3\x80\x82'
## Limited Risk-free Profit


## 有限的无风险利润
        Profit is locked in immediately when the conversion is done and it can be computed        using the following formula:

b'\xe8\xbd\xac\xe6\x8d\xa2\xe5\xae\x8c\xe6\x88\x90\xe5\x90\x8e\xe7\xab\x8b\xe5\x8d\xb3\xe9\x94\x81\xe5\xae\x9a\xe5\x88\xa9\xe6\xb6\xa6\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\xb9\xb6\xe5\x8f\xaf\xe4\xbd\xbf\xe7\x94\xa8\xe4\xbb\xa5\xe4\xb8\x8b\xe5\x85\xac\xe5\xbc\x8f\xe8\xae\xa1\xe7\xae\x97\xef\xbc\x9a'

## Example


## 例
        Suppose XYZ stock is trading at $100 in June and the JUL 100 call is priced at $4        while the JUL 100 put is priced at $3. An arbitrage trader does a conversion by        purchasing 100 shares of XYZ for $10000 while simultaneously buying a JUL 100 put        for $300 and selling a JUL 100 call for $400. The total cost to enter the trade        is $10000 + $300 - $400 = $9900.

b'\xe5\x81\x87\xe8\xae\xbe6\xe6\x9c\x88\xe4\xbb\xbdXYZ\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe7\x9a\x84\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe6\xa0\xbc\xe4\xb8\xba100\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8cJUL 100\xe5\xa5\x97\xe8\xa3\x85\xe7\x9a\x84\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe6\xa0\xbc\xe4\xb8\xba4\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe8\x80\x8cJUL 100\xe5\xa5\x97\xe8\xa3\x85\xe7\x9a\x84\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe6\xa0\xbc\xe4\xb8\xba3\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe3\x80\x82'
        Assuming XYZ stock rallies to $110 in July, the long JUL 100 put will expire worthless        while the short JUL 100 call expires in the money and is assigned. The trader then        sells his long stock for $10000 as required. Since his cost is only $9900, there        is a $100 profit.

b'\xe5\x81\x87\xe8\xae\xbeXYZ\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe5\x9c\xa87\xe6\x9c\x88\xe4\xbb\xbd\xe4\xb8\x8a\xe6\xb6\xa8\xe8\x87\xb3110\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe9\x82\xa3\xe4\xb9\x88\xe9\x95\xbf\xe6\x9c\x9fJUL 100\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe8\xb7\x8c\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe5\xb0\x86\xe6\x97\xa0\xe6\x95\x88\xef\xbc\x8c\xe8\x80\x8cJUL 100\xe7\x9a\x84\xe7\x9f\xad\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe6\xb6\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe5\xb9\xb6\xe8\xa2\xab\xe5\x88\x86\xe9\x85\x8d\xe3\x80\x82'
        If instead XYZ stock had dropped to $90 in July, the short JUL 100 call will expire        worthless while the long JUL 100 put expires in the money. The trader then exercises        the long put to sell his long stock for $10000, again netting a profit of $100.    

b'\xe7\x9b\xb8\xe5\x8f\x8d\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\xa6\x82\xe6\x9e\x9cXYZ\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe5\x9c\xa87\xe6\x9c\x88\xe4\xbb\xbd\xe8\xb7\x8c\xe8\x87\xb390\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe9\x82\xa3\xe4\xb9\x88JUL 100\xe7\x9a\x84\xe7\x9f\xad\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe6\xb6\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe5\xb0\x86\xe6\x97\xa0\xe6\x95\x88\xef\xbc\x8c\xe8\x80\x8c\xe5\xa4\x9a\xe5\xa4\xb4JUL 100\xe5\x88\x99\xe4\xbc\x9a\xe5\x9c\xa8\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x97\xb6\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe3\x80\x82'
Note: While we have covered the use of this strategy with reference to stock options, the conversion is equally applicable using ETF options, index options as well as options on futures.
