# Ratio Spread


# 比率差价
The ratio spread is a neutral strategy in options trading that involves buying a number of options    and selling more options    of the same underlying stock and expiration date    at a different strike price. It is a limited profit, unlimited risk options trading strategy that is taken when    the options trader thinks that the underlying stock will experience little    volatility in the near term.

## Limited Profit Potential


## 有限的利润潜力
        Maximum gain for the call ratio spread is limited and is made when the underlying stock price at expiration        is at the strike price of the options sold. At this price, both the written calls        expire worthless while the long call expires in the money.

## Unlimited Upside Risk


## 无限的上行风险
Loss occurs when the stock price makes a strong move to the upside beyond the upper beakeven point. There    is no limit to the maximum possible loss when implementing the call ratio spread strategy.

## Little or No Downside Risk


## 很少或没有下行风险
Any risk to the downside for the call ratio spread is limited to the debit taken to put on the spread (if    any). There may even be a profit if a credit is received when putting on the spread.

b'\xe5\xaf\xb9\xe4\xba\x8e\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe6\xb6\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\xb7\xae\xe7\x9a\x84\xe4\xbb\xbb\xe4\xbd\x95\xe4\xb8\x8b\xe8\xa1\x8c\xe9\xa3\x8e\xe9\x99\xa9\xe4\xbb\x85\xe9\x99\x90\xe4\xba\x8e\xe5\x80\x9f\xe5\x85\xa5\xe5\xb7\xae\xe4\xbb\xb7\xef\xbc\x88\xe5\xa6\x82\xe6\x9e\x9c\xe6\x9c\x89\xe7\x9a\x84\xe8\xaf\x9d\xef\xbc\x89\xe3\x80\x82'

## Example


## 例
    Suppose XYZ stock is trading at $43 in June. An options trader executes a 2:1 ratio    call spread strategy by buying a JUL 40 call for $400 and selling two JUL 45 calls for $200 each. The net debit/credit taken to enter the trade is zero.

b'\xe5\x81\x87\xe8\xae\xbeXYZ\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa86\xe6\x9c\x88\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe6\xa0\xbc\xe4\xb8\xba43\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe3\x80\x82'
        On expiration in July, if XYZ stock is trading at $45, both the JUL 45 calls expire        worthless while the long JUL 40 call expires in the money with $500 in intrinsic        value. Selling or exercising this long call will give the options trader his maximum        profit of $500.

b'\xe5\x9c\xa87\xe6\x9c\x88\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x97\xb6\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\xa6\x82\xe6\x9e\x9cXYZ\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe7\x9a\x84\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe6\xa0\xbc\xe4\xb8\xba45\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe9\x82\xa3\xe4\xb9\x88JUL 45\xe7\x9a\x84\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe6\xb6\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe5\xb0\x86\xe6\xaf\xab\xe6\x97\xa0\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\x80\xbc\xef\xbc\x8c\xe8\x80\x8c\xe9\x95\xbf\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe7\x9a\x84JUL 40\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe6\xb6\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe5\xb0\x86\xe4\xbb\xa5500\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe7\x9a\x84\xe5\x86\x85\xe5\x9c\xa8\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\x80\xbc\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe3\x80\x82'
        If XYZ stock rallies and is trading at $50 on expiration in July, all the options        will expire in the money but because the trader has written more calls than he has        bought, he will need to buy back the written calls which have increased in value.        Each JUL 45 call written is now worth $500. However, his long JUL 40 call is worth        $1000 and is just enough to offset the losses from the written calls. Therefore,        he achieves breakeven at $50.    

b'\xe5\xa6\x82\xe6\x9e\x9cXYZ\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe5\x9c\xa87\xe6\x9c\x88\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x97\xb6\xe5\x8f\x8d\xe5\xbc\xb9\xe5\xb9\xb6\xe4\xb8\x94\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe6\xa0\xbc\xe4\xb8\xba50\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe9\x82\xa3\xe4\xb9\x88\xe6\x89\x80\xe6\x9c\x89\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe9\x83\xbd\xe5\xb0\x86\xe5\x9c\xa8\xe8\xb4\xa7\xe5\xb8\x81\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xbd\x86\xe5\x9b\xa0\xe4\xb8\xba\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe8\x80\x85\xe5\xb7\xb2\xe7\xbb\x8f\xe5\x86\x99\xe4\xba\x86\xe6\xaf\x94\xe4\xbb\x96\xe4\xb9\xb0\xe7\x9a\x84\xe6\x9b\xb4\xe5\xa4\x9a\xe7\x9a\x84\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe6\xb6\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xbb\x96\xe5\xb0\x86\xe9\x9c\x80\xe8\xa6\x81\xe5\x9b\x9e\xe8\xb4\xad\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\x80\xbc\xe5\xa2\x9e\xe5\x8a\xa0\xe7\x9a\x84\xe4\xb9\xa6\xe9\x9d\xa2\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe6\xb6\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe3\x80\x82'
        Beyond $50 though, there will be no limit to the loss possible. For example, at        $60, each written JUL 45 call will be worth $1500 while his single long JUL 40 call        is only worth $2000, resulting in a loss of $1000.    

b'\xe8\xb6\x85\xe8\xbf\x8750\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\x8f\xaf\xe8\x83\xbd\xe7\x9a\x84\xe6\x8d\x9f\xe5\xa4\xb1\xe6\xb2\xa1\xe6\x9c\x89\xe9\x99\x90\xe5\x88\xb6\xe3\x80\x82'
        However, there is no downside risk to this trade. If the stock price had dropped        to $40 or below at expiration, all the options involved will expire worthless. Since        the net debit to put on this trade is zero, there is no resulting loss.

b'\xe4\xbd\x86\xe6\x98\xaf\xef\xbc\x8c\xe8\xbf\x99\xe7\xac\x94\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe6\xb2\xa1\xe6\x9c\x89\xe4\xb8\x8b\xe8\xa1\x8c\xe9\xa3\x8e\xe9\x99\xa9\xe3\x80\x82'
Note: While we have covered the use of this strategy with reference to stock options, the ratio spread is equally applicable using ETF options, index options as well as options on futures.

b'\xe6\xb3\xa8\xef\xbc\x9a\xe8\x99\xbd\xe7\x84\xb6\xe6\x88\x91\xe4\xbb\xac\xe5\xb7\xb2\xe5\x8f\x82\xe8\x80\x83\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe6\xb6\xb5\xe7\x9b\x96\xe4\xba\x86\xe6\xad\xa4\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xe7\x9a\x84\xe4\xbd\xbf\xe7\x94\xa8\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xbd\x86\xe4\xbd\xbf\xe7\x94\xa8ETF\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe6\x8c\x87\xe6\x95\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe4\xbb\xa5\xe5\x8f\x8a\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe8\xb4\xa7\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe6\x97\xb6\xef\xbc\x8c\xe6\xaf\x94\xe7\x8e\x87\xe5\xb7\xae\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\x90\x8c\xe6\xa0\xb7\xe9\x80\x82\xe7\x94\xa8\xe3\x80\x82'

## Stock Repair


## 库存维修
The call ratio spread can also be used to repair a long stock position that has been hit with an unrealized loss. This stock repair        strategy can reduce the price needed to breakeven on the long stock with    virtually no cost.

b'\xe5\x91\xbc\xe5\x8f\xab\xe6\xaf\x94\xe7\x8e\x87\xe5\xb7\xae\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe4\xb9\x9f\xe5\x8f\xaf\xe4\xbb\xa5\xe7\x94\xa8\xe4\xba\x8e\xe4\xbf\xae\xe5\xa4\x8d\xe5\xb7\xb2\xe7\xbb\x8f\xe9\x81\xad\xe5\x8f\x97\xe6\x9c\xaa\xe5\xae\x9e\xe7\x8e\xb0\xe6\x8d\x9f\xe5\xa4\xb1\xe7\x9a\x84\xe9\x95\xbf\xe4\xbb\x93\xe4\xbd\x8d\xe3\x80\x82'
## Put Ratio Spread


## 投入比率差价
The ratio spread can also be constructed using puts. The put ratio spread is similar    to the call ratio spread strategy but has a slightly more bullish and less bearish    risk profile.
