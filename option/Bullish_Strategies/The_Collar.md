# The Collar Strategy


# 领子战略
A collar is an options trading strategy that is constructed by holding shares of the underlying stock while simultaneously buying         protective puts and selling call options    against that holding. The puts and the calls are both         out-of-the-money options having the same expiration month and must be equal    in number of contracts. 

b'\xe9\xa2\x86\xe5\xad\x90\xe6\x98\xaf\xe4\xb8\x80\xe7\xa7\x8d\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xef\xbc\x8c\xe9\x80\x9a\xe8\xbf\x87\xe6\x8c\x81\xe6\x9c\x89\xe7\x9b\xb8\xe5\x85\xb3\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe7\x9a\x84\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe5\x90\x8c\xe6\x97\xb6\xe8\xb4\xad\xe4\xb9\xb0\xe4\xbf\x9d\xe6\x8a\xa4\xe6\x80\xa7\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe8\xb7\x8c\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe5\x92\x8c\xe5\x8d\x96\xe5\x87\xba\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe6\xb6\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe6\x9d\xa5\xe6\x9e\x84\xe5\xbb\xba\xe3\x80\x82'
Technically, the collar strategy is the equivalent of a     out-of-the-money covered call strategy with the purchase of an additional protective put.

b'\xe4\xbb\x8e\xe6\x8a\x80\xe6\x9c\xaf\xe4\xb8\x8a\xe8\xae\xb2\xef\xbc\x8c\xe9\xa2\x86\xe5\xad\x90\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xe7\x9b\xb8\xe5\xbd\x93\xe4\xba\x8e\xe9\x80\x9a\xe8\xbf\x87\xe8\xb4\xad\xe4\xb9\xb0\xe9\xa2\x9d\xe5\xa4\x96\xe7\x9a\x84\xe4\xbf\x9d\xe6\x8a\xa4\xe6\x80\xa7\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe8\xb7\x8c\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe8\x80\x8c\xe5\xae\x9e\xe7\x8e\xb0\xe7\x9a\x84\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\xa4\x96\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe8\xb7\x8c\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xe3\x80\x82'
The collar is a good strategy to use if the options trader is writing covered calls to earn premiums but wish to protect himself from an unexpected sharp drop in the price of the underlying security.

b'\xe5\xa6\x82\xe6\x9e\x9c\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe8\x80\x85\xe6\xad\xa3\xe5\x9c\xa8\xe7\xbc\x96\xe5\x86\x99\xe6\x89\xbf\xe4\xbf\x9d\xe7\x9a\x84\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe6\xb6\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xbd\x86\xe5\xb8\x8c\xe6\x9c\x9b\xe4\xbf\x9d\xe6\x8a\xa4\xe8\x87\xaa\xe5\xb7\xb1\xe5\x85\x8d\xe5\x8f\x97\xe5\x9f\xba\xe7\xa1\x80\xe8\xaf\x81\xe5\x88\xb8\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe6\xa0\xbc\xe6\x84\x8f\xe5\xa4\x96\xe5\xa4\xa7\xe5\xb9\x85\xe4\xb8\x8b\xe8\xb7\x8c\xe7\x9a\x84\xe5\xbd\xb1\xe5\x93\x8d\xef\xbc\x8c\xe9\x82\xa3\xe4\xb9\x88\xe9\xa2\x86\xe5\xad\x90\xe6\x98\xaf\xe4\xb8\x80\xe4\xb8\xaa\xe5\xbe\x88\xe5\xa5\xbd\xe7\x9a\x84\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xe3\x80\x82'

## Example


## 例
Suppose an options trader is holding 100 shares of the stock XYZ currently trading at $48 in June. He decides to establish a collar by writing a JUL 50 covered call for $2 while simultaneously purchases a JUL 45 put for $1. 

b'\xe5\x81\x87\xe8\xae\xbe\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe5\x95\x86\xe6\x8c\x81\xe6\x9c\x89XZ\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe7\x9a\x84100\xe8\x82\xa1\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xef\xbc\x8c\xe7\x9b\xae\xe5\x89\x8d6\xe6\x9c\x88\xe4\xba\xa4\xe6\x98\x93\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe6\xa0\xbc\xe4\xb8\xba48\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe3\x80\x82'
Since he pays $4800 for the 100 shares of XYZ, another $100 for the put but receives $200 for selling the call option, his total investment is $4700.

b'\xe7\x94\xb1\xe4\xba\x8e\xe4\xbb\x96\xe4\xb8\xba100\xe8\x82\xa1XYZ\xe6\x94\xaf\xe4\xbb\x98\xe4\xba\x864800\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\x8f\xa6\xe5\xa4\x96100\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe7\x9a\x84\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe8\xb7\x8c\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xbd\x86\xe6\x94\xb6\xe5\x88\xb0200\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe7\x9a\x84\xe5\x8d\x96\xe5\x87\xba\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe6\xb6\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xbb\x96\xe7\x9a\x84\xe6\x80\xbb\xe6\x8a\x95\xe8\xb5\x84\xe6\x98\xaf4700\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe3\x80\x82'
On expiration date, the stock had rallied by 5 points to $53. Since the striking price of $50 for the call option is lower than the trading price of the stock, the call is assigned and the trader sells the shares for $5000, resulting in a $300 profit ($5000 minus $4700 original investment).

b'\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x97\xa5\xef\xbc\x8c\xe8\x82\xa1\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe4\xb8\x8a\xe6\xb6\xa85\xe7\x82\xb9\xe8\x87\xb353\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe3\x80\x82'
However, what happens should the stock price had gone down 5 points to $43 instead? Let's take a look.

b'\xe4\xbd\x86\xe6\x98\xaf\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\xa6\x82\xe6\x9e\x9c\xe8\x82\xa1\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe4\xb8\x8b\xe8\xb7\x8c5\xe7\x82\xb9\xe8\x87\xb343\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xbc\x9a\xe5\x8f\x91\xe7\x94\x9f\xe4\xbb\x80\xe4\xb9\x88\xe5\x91\xa2\xef\xbc\x9f'
At $43, the call writer would have had incurred a paper loss of $500 for holding the 100 shares of XYZ but because of the JUL 45 protective put, he is able to sell his shares for $4500 instead of $4300. Thus, his net loss is limited to only $200 ($4500 minus $4700 original investment).

b'\xe5\x8f\xaa\xe9\x9c\x8043\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe7\x94\xb5\xe8\xaf\x9d\xe6\x92\xb0\xe7\xa8\xbf\xe4\xba\xba\xe5\x9b\xa0\xe6\x8c\x81\xe6\x9c\x89100\xe8\x82\xa1XYZ\xe8\x80\x8c\xe9\x81\xad\xe5\x8f\x97\xe7\xba\xb8\xe8\xb4\xa8\xe6\x8d\x9f\xe5\xa4\xb1500\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xbd\x86\xe7\x94\xb1\xe4\xba\x8eJUL 45\xe4\xbf\x9d\xe6\x8a\xa4\xe6\x80\xa7\xe7\x9c\x8b\xe8\xb7\x8c\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xbb\x96\xe8\x83\xbd\xe5\xa4\x9f\xe4\xbb\xa54500\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe8\x80\x8c\xe4\xb8\x8d\xe6\x98\xaf4300\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe7\x9a\x84\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe6\xa0\xbc\xe5\x8d\x96\xe5\x87\xba\xe4\xbb\x96\xe7\x9a\x84\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe3\x80\x82'
Had the stock price remain stable at $48 at expiration, he will still net a paper gain of $100 since he only paid a total of $4700 to acquire $4800 worth of stock.

b'\xe5\xa6\x82\xe6\x9e\x9c\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe6\xa0\xbc\xe5\x9c\xa8\xe5\x88\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x97\xb6\xe4\xbf\x9d\xe6\x8c\x81\xe7\xa8\xb3\xe5\xae\x9a\xe5\x9c\xa848\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xbb\x96\xe4\xbb\x8d\xe7\x84\xb6\xe5\x8f\xaf\xe4\xbb\xa5\xe8\x8e\xb7\xe5\xbe\x97100\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe7\x9a\x84\xe7\xba\xb8\xe9\x9d\xa2\xe6\x94\xb6\xe7\x9b\x8a\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\x9b\xa0\xe4\xb8\xba\xe4\xbb\x96\xe5\x8f\xaa\xe9\x9c\x80\xe8\xa6\x81\xe6\x94\xaf\xe4\xbb\x984700\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe6\x9d\xa5\xe8\x8e\xb7\xe5\xbe\x97\xe4\xbb\xb7\xe5\x80\xbc4800\xe7\xbe\x8e\xe5\x85\x83\xe7\x9a\x84\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe3\x80\x82'
Note: While we have covered the use of this strategy with reference to stock options, the collar strategy is equally applicable using ETF options, index options as well as options on futures.

b'\xe6\xb3\xa8\xe6\x84\x8f\xef\xbc\x9a\xe8\x99\xbd\xe7\x84\xb6\xe6\x88\x91\xe4\xbb\xac\xe5\xb7\xb2\xe7\xbb\x8f\xe5\x8f\x82\xe8\x80\x83\xe8\x82\xa1\xe7\xa5\xa8\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe6\xb6\xb5\xe7\x9b\x96\xe4\xba\x86\xe8\xaf\xa5\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xe7\x9a\x84\xe4\xbd\xbf\xe7\x94\xa8\xef\xbc\x8c\xe4\xbd\x86\xe6\x98\xaf\xe4\xbd\xbf\xe7\x94\xa8ETF\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe6\x8c\x87\xe6\x95\xb0\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xe4\xbb\xa5\xe5\x8f\x8a\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe8\xb4\xa7\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe6\x9d\x83\xef\xbc\x8c\xe5\xa5\x97\xe6\x9c\x9f\xe7\xad\x96\xe7\x95\xa5\xe5\x90\x8c\xe6\xa0\xb7\xe9\x80\x82\xe7\x94\xa8\xe3\x80\x82'

## Summary


## 概要
The beauty of using a collar strategy is that you know, right from the start, the potential losses and gains on a trade. While your returns are likely to be somewhat muted in an explosive bull market due to selling the call, on the flip side, should the stock heads south, you'll have the comfort of knowing you're protected.
